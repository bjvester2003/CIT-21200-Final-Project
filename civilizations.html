Civilizations

Aztecs

This Central American civiliation is heavily dedicated to the development of their infantry. Due to the technical limitations, the Aztecs posses weaker archers. Additionally, they have no horses to form a calvary.

The Aztecs receive bonuses to monks and villagers

Jaguar Warrior

The unique unit of the Aztecs, the Jaguar warrior is adapt at countering enemy infantry.

The Britons offer substantial bonuses to ranged units.

Byzantines

The Byzantines are a versitle civilization. They posses bonuses to camel units, ships, walls, and counter units.

Poles

The Poles offer bonuses to villagers, such as additional health regeneratuon and additional gold generation.

In addition to a unique unit, the Poles possess a unique building. The folwark acts as an upgraded mill, returning an additional 10% of collected food.

Japanese

Japan offers bonuses to both its economy and its navy. Resource depots are cheaper to establish and galleys can see 50% farther.

Additionally, infantry attack faster.
