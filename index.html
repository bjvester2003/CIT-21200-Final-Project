Game History

Age of Empires II was first released in 1999. Over the two decades since its release, AOEII has received numerous updates and DLC expansions.

In 2019, on AOEII's twentieth anniverisery, a Definitive Edition was released containing new civilizations, units, and a graphical overhaul.

Gameplay Overview

Age of Empires is a game of strategy and unit management on a massive digital scale. To succeed, you must manage your economy and military while fending off raids from your opponents.

As you command your armies and build your empire, direct your villagers to gather resources for expansion. Gold, wood, food, and stone are all important, but you will need to prioritize to win.
