History

The Great Khan

In the 13th century AD, Genghis Kahn conquered much of Asia and Europe with his great horde of Mongols. For twenty-one years, the Great Khan ruled over one of the largest empires the world had ever seen.

In Age of Empires, players command the Khan's forces as he struggles to unite the tribes of Mongolia. From there, make ready to break through the Great Wall and threaten all of China.

The Maid of Orléons

Saint Joan of Arc was hailed as a visionary in France during the 1400s. Supposedly, the Maid of Orléons had received messages from the Saints Michael, Catherine, and Margaret. Though only a teenager, Joan of Arc led the armies of France in defending the city of Orléons from English invaders.

In Age of Empires, players can take command of Joan of Arc as she leads the defens of Orléons.

The Sultan of Arabia

Saladin was very wise in the ways of war. Early in his life he gained experience in the army of his uncle, a renowned battlefield commander. In time, however, Saladin became a great leader in his own right. Over the course of several decades, Saladin fought many battles with european crusaders. At times, Saladin even captured Jerusalem and faced off against the legendary Richard the Lionheart.

In Age of Empires, players lead Saladin's army as he defends Cairo, Sieges Jerusalem, and stares into the jaws of the Great Enlish Lion.
